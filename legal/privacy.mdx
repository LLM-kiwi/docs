This Privacy Policy explains how LLM Kiwi ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our API services and website.

## Data Controller

LLM Kiwi is the data controller for the personal data collected through our services. For GDPR inquiries, contact us at **privacy@llm.kiwi**.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

| Data Category | Purpose | Retention |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Email address | Account identification, communication | Account lifetime |
| Name | Display purposes | Account lifetime |
| Payment details | Billing (processed by Stripe) | Per payment processor policy |

### Usage Data

| Data Category | Purpose | Retention |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| API requests | Service delivery, abuse prevention | 30 days |
| Request metadata | Analytics, debugging | 30 days |
| Error logs | Service improvement | 14 days |

### Prompt and Response Data

<Warning>
  We **do not** store prompt content or model responses beyond the time required to process requests. Your conversations are not used for model training.
</Warning>

## Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data
- **Rectify** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data (right to be forgotten)
- **Restrict** processing
- **Data portability** (export your data)
- **Object** to processing
- **Withdraw consent** at any time

To exercise these rights, email **privacy@llm.kiwi**.

## International Transfers

Data may be transferred to the United States for processing. We use Standard Contractual Clauses to ensure adequate protection.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- TLS encryption for all data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be notified via email.
