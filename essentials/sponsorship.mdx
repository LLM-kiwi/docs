## Application Process

### Step 1: Prepare Documentation

Gather the following based on your category:

**Students:**
- Proof of enrollment (student ID, enrollment letter)
- Brief description of your project

**Educators:**
- Faculty ID or employment verification
- Course syllabus or curriculum outline

**Open Source Maintainers:**
- Link to public repository with your contributions
- Evidence of active maintenance (commits, issues)

**Non-Profits:**
- 501(c)(3) or equivalent registration
- Description of AI use case

### Step 2: Submit Application

Send your application to **sponsorship@llm.kiwi** with:

1. Your name and email
2. Category you're applying under
3. Documentation listed above
4. Brief description of how you'll use llm.kiwi

### Step 3: Review Period

Applications are reviewed within **5 business days**. You'll receive:

- Approval email with Pro access activation
- Or a request for additional information
- Or a rejection with explanation

## Expectations

Sponsored users agree to:

- Use the API for educational, research, or non-commercial purposes
- Attribute llm.kiwi in public projects (optional but appreciated)
- Not resell or redistribute API access
- Maintain active use (accounts inactive for 90+ days may be reviewed)

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="How long does sponsorship last?">
  Initial sponsorship is for 12 months. Renewal is available upon request with proof of continued eligibility.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use sponsored access for commercial projects?">
  No, sponsored access is for educational, research, and non-commercial use only. Please upgrade to a paid plan for commercial applications.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if I graduate or leave my institution?">
  Your sponsorship remains active until the end of your current term. You can apply for a different category (e.g., open source) if eligible.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I sponsor my students?">
  Yes! Educators can request bulk sponsorships for their students. Contact us at sponsorship@llm.kiwi with your class size and curriculum details.
</Accordion>
